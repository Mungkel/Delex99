How To Build The Delex99



Required parts:

- Microcontroller with more 25 digital pins or more, I used a teensy 4.1

- either a large 3d printer or access to a 3d printing service like PCBway (not sponsored, there other services) or a CNC if you're really fancy and want to make it from metal

- a soldering iron and cable (best if multiple different colors)

- 101 switches and keycaps fitting to your desired design

- a cable fitting to your Microcontroller or adapter
